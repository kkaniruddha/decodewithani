🌍 Why This Matters Right Now

We are living through one of the most significant technological shifts in human history. Not since the internet — maybe not since the printing press — has a single technology had the potential to change how we work, learn, create, communicate, and govern ourselves as fundamentally as AI is about to.

And unlike the internet, this one is moving fast. Uncomfortably fast. ChatGPT went from launch to 100 million users in two months. That's not a product launch — that's a rupture. The companies building this technology are making decisions right now, in boardrooms and government offices, that will shape the world your children grow up in.

You deserve to understand what those decisions are. Not because you need to become a tech expert — but because informed people make better decisions about their own lives, their careers, their businesses, and the society they want to live in.

🔑 The Most Important Thing I've Learned

AI is not going to replace people who understand it. The real divide in the next decade won't be between smart people and less smart people. It will be between people who understand AI and people who don't. This blog is my contribution to closing that gap.
