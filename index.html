About Me

The person behind
DecodeAI with Ani

Hey! I'm Ani — an AI writer and educator on a mission to make artificial intelligence understandable for everyone, not just engineers and tech bros.

I got tired of reading AI content that was either too technical to follow or too shallow to be useful. So I started DecodeAI with Ani — a space where real people can learn about AI through storytelling, plain-English explainers, honest tool reviews, and grounded opinions.

I've spent months researching AI company histories, testing tools, following the news, and translating all of it into content that doesn't require a PhD to understand. My interactive AI Tools Cheat Sheet has already helped thousands of people navigate the AI landscape.

Whether you're a student, a professional, a business owner, or simply a curious human — you belong here.
