Before We Start

You Are About to Make Something That Didn't Exist 5 Minutes Ago

Here's something that would have sounded like science fiction ten years ago:

You type a sentence. A computer reads it. And within seconds — it generates a full, beautiful, detailed image based entirely on your words. Not a stock photo. Not a Google search result. An original piece of visual art — made from scratch, just for you, just from what you typed.

That's AI art. And today, you're going to make some.

💡 What Is an AI Art Prompt?

A prompt is simply the text you type to tell the AI what image to create. It's your instruction. Your description. Your imagination — typed out.

The better your prompt, the better your image. And by the end of this post, you'll know exactly how to write one that works — even if you've never done this before.

Think of it like ordering food at a restaurant. The more specific your order, the more likely you get exactly what you want. "I want something good" gets you something random. "A thin-crust margherita pizza with extra basil and light cheese" gets you exactly what you imagined.

AI art works the same way.

Step 2 of 5

The Secret Formula — How to Write a Prompt That Actually Works

Most beginners type one or two words and get a blurry, confusing image. Then they think "AI art doesn't work." It does — you just need to give the AI more to work with.

Here is the exact formula that works every single time:

🧩 The 5-Part Prompt Formula

Use all five parts together and your image quality will jump dramatically.

Subject
+
Setting / Place
+
Mood / Lighting
+
Art Style
+
Extra Details
=
🎨 Great Image
📝 Formula in Action — Example

A young woman reading a book [Subject]
sitting in a cozy café in Paris [Setting]
on a rainy evening, warm golden light [Mood]
in the style of a watercolour painting [Art Style]
soft colours, detailed, cinematic atmosphere [Extra Details]

Good Prompt vs Bad Prompt — See the Difference

❌ Weak Prompt

a cat

✅ Strong Prompt

a fluffy orange cat sitting on a windowsill at sunset, looking outside at the rain, cozy warm lighting, photorealistic style, highly detailed

❌ Weak Prompt

mountain view

✅ Strong Prompt

a dramatic snow-capped mountain range at golden hour, a tiny wooden cabin in the foreground with smoke from the chimney, oil painting style, epic landscape, deep blue sky

💡 The Golden Rule of AI Prompts

Describe the image the way you'd describe it to a friend who cannot see it — be specific about what's in it, where it's set, what it feels like, and what style it should look like. More detail = better image. Always.

The single easiest way to dramatically improve your AI images is to add an art style to your prompt. Here are the best ones — just copy and paste any of these into your prompt.

🎨 Realistic / Photography Look

✏️ Sketch / Illustration

...pencil sketch style, hand-drawn illustration, clean lines, minimal shading, sketchbook aesthetic

...anime art style, Studio Ghibli inspired, soft pastel colours, detailed background, cel shading

🔮 Fantasy / Cinematic

...epic fantasy art, cinematic wide shot, dramatic lighting, concept art, detailed environment, magical atmosphere

Step 5 of 5

Your Image Didn't Come Out Right? Here's How to Fix It

Your first image might not be perfect — and that is completely normal. AI art is a conversation, not a one-shot command. Here's how to improve it.

🔧 The 4 Fixes That Work Every Time

Too blurry or low quality? Add these words to your prompt: "highly detailed, sharp focus, 4K, professional quality"

Wrong mood or feeling? Add lighting words: "golden hour", "blue hour", "neon lights", "soft morning light", "dramatic shadows", "warm candlelight"

Looks too random or messy? Add style words: "clean composition", "rule of thirds", "minimalist", "simple background", "centred subject"

Not detailed enough? Add: "intricate details", "rich textures", "ultra realistic", "8K resolution", "award winning photography"

🔁 The Most Important Thing to Know

Generate it multiple times. Even with the exact same prompt, AI generates a different image every time. If you don't love the first result — hit generate again. And again. Most AI artists generate 5–20 versions before choosing their favourite. That's not failure — that's the process.
